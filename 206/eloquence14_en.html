Welcome!

We are going to give you a report on the final of the eloquence contest of Thursday, April 14th, 2022 which took place at the USPN.

Presentation of the event

Our communication teachers began by introducing us to the art of being eloquent.

While stressing the importance of this skill.

In order to know the running order of each participant, they proceeded to a draw.

Passage of participants

After the order of performance had been decided, the participants passed by and they answered the following issue:
The metaverse, is life elsewhere ?

Each participant had his own theme and the performances stood out from each other.
Among the themes presented, we were able to find comedy, justice, scandal, storytelling but also slam.

Despite the large number of people in the room, the young students kept their cool and performed remarkably despite their lack of experience.

Résultat

The teachers were pleasantly surprised to see such a high level this year compared to previous years.

Choosing the winners was very difficult as each participant stood out in their own way.

The teachers found it difficult to decide between them.


In the third place , we find Sylla who knew how to stand out a little more compare to the other candidates.
Thanks to its oral ease but also by its risk-taking in which he knew how to mix comedie and opinion in relation to the controversial subject of the metaverse.

By a particular speech, he won the attention of the audience and the teachers by making us laugh and think at the same time.

This was probably the participant who persuaded the students the most.


In this eloquence contest, we had an unexpected result!
In view of the remarkable performances of the participants, the jury could not decide between 2 participants to decide the winner of the competition.

So we have as winners, both first ex-aequo, Tinhinane and Kais.

They were able to lead the theme of the metaverse in a legal debate Both, had the same theme and knew how to have a different approach from each other, which led them to victory.
