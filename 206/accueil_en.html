The Eloquence Contest, or the art of convincing and moving

In June 2022, the University Sorbonne Paris Nord will present you a great day on eloquence.

Spirit of the Competition

Eloquence is not grandiloquence, brilliance, talkativeness, recitation.

It is the art of mastering one's thoughts in action.

The Eloquence Contest is therefore intended to evaluate the candidate's ability to

mobilize oratory techniques to leave a lasting impression

in the minds of its audience.

Friday, June 17, 2022, BIG DAY OF ELOQUENCE at the University Sorbonne Paris 13.

Come and live unforgettable moments with us!
